About Us

A yet another scholastic venture of DPS group and an offshoot of the Chandigarh Campus, D.P.S. World School, Sector 28, Panchkula imparts supreme education, presents an enriching experience, and conditions future leaders of the globe. The school has been awarded the prestigious “IGBC Platinum Ranking” by the Indian Green Building Council (IGBC) and follows the CBSE Curriculum. Stretching from setting the state-of-the-art infrastructure & ultra-modern learning spaces to conceiving a trailblazing and promising curriculum, the school aspires to maximize the efficiency of learners and broaden their horizons to attain self-direction as they progress in life. For the same, DPS International School, Panchkula was awarded the “Outstanding Concrete Structure Award 2021” (In School Category) by Indian Concrete Institute in collaboration with Aditya Birla Group. Furthermore, the school provides a highly convivial environment to the pupils, by means of which, it stands as a methodical imparter of futuristic, interdisciplinary and global education. With its faculty members possessing scientific, progressive and technological bent of mind, the school successfully meets the contemporaneous as well as futuristic educational needs of the students. The Panchkula Campus of DPS World School solemnly carries on and upholds the meritorious vision of the educational revolutionist and its judicious establisher, Dr Madhu Chitkara, who holds absolute and admirable credence in the power of education and works resolutely to impart the same with a high emphasis on contemporary needs. The school further aims to devotedly tread the enlightening path taken by Dr Niyati Chitkara, its insightful mentor, with the tireless efforts of whom the DPS Family continues to expand and progress increasingly.
